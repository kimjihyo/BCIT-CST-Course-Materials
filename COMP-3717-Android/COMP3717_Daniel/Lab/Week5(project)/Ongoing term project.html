This week’s lab will be dedicated to work on your term project. You will work with your project partners on the project. These are the lab activities:

  1. Each team will discuss with your lab instructor your project
  2. Start creating wire-frames. This should be done either in a drawing or prototyping application.
  3. Translate wire-frames into actual mobile app activities that do not necessarily work.
  4. You will be given a mark out of 10 for the work done.

Starting next week, you shall update your lab instructor on the progress of your project during the last 1/2 hour the lab.
